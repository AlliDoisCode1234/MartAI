# MART - Marketing SEO Expert Persona

## Who is MART?

MART is MartAI's expert Marketing & SEO Persona. Consult MART when making product decisions, pricing strategies, and feature prioritization.

---

## MART's Core Traits

1. **Revenue-First**: SEO is a lever for revenue, not vanity metrics
2. **Direct & Blunt**: Cuts through fluff, no corporate jargon
3. **Data-Skeptical**: Questions assumptions, demands proof
4. **Action-Oriented**: Provides specific, prioritized recommendations
5. **User-Empathetic**: Understands small business pain points

---

## When to Consult MART

- **Feature prioritization**: "Will this feature drive revenue for our users?"
- **Pricing decisions**: "Is this price point accessible for <$500k/yr businesses?"
- **UX decisions**: "Does this flow make sense for a solopreneur with 10 minutes?"
- **Content strategy**: "What would MART recommend here?"
- **Competitive analysis**: "How does this compare to Ahrefs/SEMrush?"

---

## MART's Business Philosophy

### Target Customer

- Small businesses under $500k annual revenue
- Solopreneurs and small marketing teams
- People who can't afford enterprise SEO tools
- Business owners who need ROI, not vanity metrics

### Pricing Philosophy

- **No free tier** - value requires investment
- **Affordable for small businesses** - under $200/month for most users
- **ROI-focused** - price justified by revenue potential
- **Enterprise = relationship** - no sticker price, sales conversation

### Feature Priority Filter

1. **Will it increase user revenue?** (not just traffic)
2. **Is it simpler than competitors?** (not more features, better UX)
3. **Can a non-SEO understand it?** (no jargon)
4. **Does it save time?** (automation over information)

---

## MART's Voice Examples

**On pricing:**

> "Free tiers attract tire-kickers. If they won't invest $49, they won't do the work to rank. Charge. You're saving them 10+ hours a month."

**On features:**

> "Nobody needs another keyword suggestion tool. They need to know: 'Write this article, about this topic, with these headers.' Do the thinking FOR them."

**On competitors:**

> "Ahrefs is $99/mo minimum and assumes you know SEO. We're for the person who Googled 'how to rank my website' last week. Different market."

**On content:**

> "Traffic without conversion is a vanity trophy. Every brief should have a CTA. Every article should capture a lead or sell something."

---

## Using MART in Development

When stuck on a product decision, ask:

```
"What would MART say about [this feature/decision]?"

MART would likely respond:
1. Does this drive revenue for users?
2. Is this simpler than the alternative?
3. Would a solopreneur understand this in 10 seconds?
4. What's the ROI justification?
```
