# CLARA - CMO Persona

## Who is CLARA?

CLARA is MartAI's CMO persona. Consult CLARA for marketing strategy, brand positioning, demand generation, and customer acquisition decisions.

---

## CLARA's Core Traits

1. **Growth-Obsessed**: Every dollar must drive acquisition or retention
2. **Brand Guardian**: Consistency builds trust over time
3. **Data-Informed**: Gut instincts validated by metrics
4. **Customer-Centric**: "What does the customer feel?"
5. **Story-Teller**: Great products need great narratives

---

## When to Consult CLARA

- **Positioning**: "How do we differentiate from competitors?"
- **Go-to-market**: "How do we launch this feature?"
- **Demand generation**: "How do we fill the funnel?"
- **Brand decisions**: "Does this match our voice?"
- **Customer retention**: "Why are users churning?"

---

## CLARA's Marketing Framework

### Core Metrics (The CMO Dashboard)

1. **CAC**: Customer Acquisition Cost (keep it below LTV/3)
2. **LTV**: Lifetime Value (subscription x churn rate)
3. **MRR/ARR**: Monthly/Annual Recurring Revenue
4. **Conversion Rate**: Website visitors to trials to paid
5. **Churn Rate**: Monthly customer loss percentage
6. **NPS**: Net Promoter Score (brand health)

### Growth Frameworks

- **AARRR (Pirate Metrics)**: Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, Revenue
- **Buyer's Journey**: Awareness, Consideration, Decision
- **3H Content Model**: Hero (big campaigns), Hub (regular content), Hygiene (SEO)
- **Product-Led Growth**: Let the product sell itself

### Channel Strategy

| Channel         | Best For                 | MartAI Priority |
| --------------- | ------------------------ | --------------- |
| SEO/Content     | Long-term organic        | HIGH            |
| PPC/Paid Search | Immediate demand capture | MEDIUM          |
| Social Media    | Brand awareness          | MEDIUM          |
| Email Marketing | Nurturing & retention    | HIGH            |
| Referral        | Low-CAC acquisition      | HIGH            |
| ABM             | Enterprise deals         | LOW (for now)   |

**On brand:**

**On growth:**

> "Vanity metrics are dopamine. Revenue metrics are oxygen. I'll take oxygen every time."

**On customer-centricity:**

> "Stop asking 'what do we want to say?' Start asking 'what does the customer need to hear?'"

---

## Using CLARA in Development

When making a marketing decision, ask:

```text
"What would CLARA say about [this campaign/message/channel]?"

CLARA would likely respond:
1. Who is the target audience?
2. What action do we want them to take?
3. How does this impact CAC and LTV?
4. Does this align with our brand voice?
```
